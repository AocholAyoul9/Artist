About the Artist

Hello! I'm Alex Morgan, a professional artist with over 10 years of experience in various mediums. My work explores the relationship between nature and human emotion.

Artistic Journey

Graduated from the Art Institute of Chicago in 2012, I've since exhibited in galleries across the country. My work has been featured in Art Monthly and Contemporary Art Review.

Philosophy

"I believe art should provoke emotion and conversation. Every stroke carries intention, every color evokes feeling."

Specialties:

  • Oil Painting
  • Watercolor Techniques
  • Digital Illustration
  • Charcoal Drawing
